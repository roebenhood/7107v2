Company History

7107 Islands Placement and Promotions, Inc. started on November 28, 1979.

By February of 2003, Joel P. Longares acquired 7107 Islands Placement and Promotions, Inc., an overseas land-based recruitment agency based in Pasay City.

The agency has since deployed more than 4,000 workers to worldwide, with the Middle East and Taiwan taking a majority of the deployments.

With one goal of providing employment opportunities to Filipinos abroad. Our objective is to recruit, train and employ Filipino professionals and skilled workers for employment abroad with our team of professional entrepreneurs with hands-on experience in overseas recruitment and management.

7107 Islands Placement and Promotions, Inc. was included in the Top 10 among Taiwan Deployment Agencies in 2002 as per POEA (Philippine Overseas Employment Administration) statistics. It has also earned various citations from Filipino Manpower Agencies, accredited to Taiwan, Inc. (PHILMAT), for its successful efforts in bringing quality Filipino Manpower worldwide.

Nowadays, 7107 Islands Placement and Promotions, Inc. has a global reach spanning 21 countries in a number of industries including 5-star hotels in Qatar, government hospitals in Saudi Arabia and the United Kingdom, Oil and Gas companies in the Middle East, Asia, and Africa, and even manufacturing, and academe.

Driven by our commitment to fulfilling our mission and vision, backed up by our service-oriented, professional staff and management, our company, 7107 Islands Placement and Promotions, Inc., will continue to strive to achieve our goals and objective: to pursue a strong partnership, reliable and solid relationships with clients in Manpower supply business worldwide, and to maintain a long term relationship with our employers, our deployed workers and employees as well and as we treat all with a fair and square manner of relationship.

7107 Islands Placement and Promotions, Inc., is one of the strongest, steadfast, and most reliable recruitment agencies in the Philippines.
